98.5 KTK 2015 Inspire Women Award
Celebrating Women's History Month

"Patrice Fletcher started Joshua Joel SMA Life, Inc. in 2012 to raise money, awareness and research for Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA), the number one genetic cause of death of infants and toddlers. After losing her own, when he was only five to the disease in 2013, she continues to facilitate help to parents and the medical community through education, Patrice is an inspiration to everyone for using her own experience, pain and loss to inspire others to value life and give back to their community."
