Patrice Fletcher

Event Coordinator

“When it rains look for rainbows, when its dark look for stars”

As her quote suggests, Patrice is the type of positive and encouraging person that make her ideal for engaging with event guests and problem solving on the W&Co. team. Her active role in family-life and her daughter's dance troupe keep her very busy and she likes to get outdoors to enjoy a nice relaxing walk when it's time to let off steam. After 15 years in retail management Patrice decided to make a career change that would utilize her excellent customer service, and organization skills in a whole new way. And so she hit the books, and graduated from the University of Florida with a speculation in Event Management! She's always willing to offer a helping hand, and take-on the heavy lifting. We sometimes need to remind her to take a break!
